Tribute to the best cat in the World

Sally doesn't agree with you

Sally was found on September 10th in Tamagawa, Japan.

Sally was sick and blind a few centimetres away to fall down a bridge when Jack and Steph found her. They took care of her and she became a truly adorable, funny and loyal cat.

After a few months living with Jack and Steph, Sally became sick again. She had to visit the vet twice a week for six months in order to recover from her illness. Even though she was sick, she did not seem so, she liked to play with her friend Scraps and climb everywhere. She took a plane to Canada and lived there happily with Steph for 2 years. She had a heart attack on May 21st and was diagnosed a terminal heart disease on May 24th.

In the morning of June 9th around 8:00am, when Steph was about to go to the office. Sally made a horrible sound, threw up and fell on her side in her own vomit. After seeing the state of her beloved cat decreasing each day for 3 weeks, this event was a big shock for Steph. Later that day Sally was brought to the vet in order to put an end to her suffering.

She was buried in the backyard and her family planted a lilac tree on her tomb so she would blossom every June.
